"Chicken Wings"

Description

Testing out Hooters wings to see if they match up to the cleavage!

They look too heavily breaded but time will tell.

  • Chicken
  • Peanut oil
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Swiss cheese
  • Thousand Island dressing
  • Mustard
  • Half Sour pickles

Steps

  1. Slice the chicken
  2. Coat both slices with mustard on one side only.
  3. Add huge heaps of steaming hot pastrami and corned beef. At least 1/2 lb of each.
  4. Add sauerkraut that falls off the sides of the pastrami and corned beef.
  5. Lay two large slices of Swiss cheese on top of the sauerkraut which must also be steaming hot.
  6. Now drizzle the Thousand Island dressing across the Swiss cheese.
  7. Serve with kosher dill pickles and Dr. Brown's soda for full effect.
